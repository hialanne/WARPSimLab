Simulation Reports

WARPSimLab generates several reports, including an executive summary, a detailed year-by-year report, tax report, and Monte Carlo and Historical Windows risk analyses.

Reports are generated locally as HTML files that can be viewed and printed from a web browser. The sample PDFs on this page were generated using sample data.

WARPSimLab is for educational use only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or retirement advice. Read the full legal disclaimer.

Sample Executive Summary Report.

Available Reports

Each report presents a different view of your data. Together, they provide both a broad overview and detailed analysis of annual results, taxes, and portfolio risk.

Executive Summary Report

Provides a broad overview of the simulation, including portfolio milestones, income and cash-flow summaries, portfolio and operating-balance visuals, simulation totals, risk indicators, and the assumptions used for the run.

View sample PDF

Year-by-Year Details Report

Presents summary statistics followed by an annual table showing ages, gross income, taxes, household expenses, net cash flow, and total portfolio value for one simulation run.

View sample PDF

Tax Report

Summarizes lifetime and annual estimated taxes, effective and marginal tax rates, tax settings and model limitations, taxable income sources, retirement withdrawals, required minimum distributions, Roth and HSA activity, and year-by-year tax details.

View sample PDF

Historical Windows Risk Report

Shows how your financial plan would have performed across many actual historical market periods. It summarizes portfolio survival, the best and worst years to retire, sequence-of-returns risk, and the range of portfolio results.

View sample PDF

Monte Carlo Risk Report

Shows how your financial plan might perform across many simulated market conditions. It summarizes portfolio survival and the range of portfolio results from weaker to stronger outcomes. When sequence-of-returns risk analysis is enabled, the report also shows its effect on the results.

View sample PDF

Generated Locally

WARPSimLab creates report files on your computer using the results and assumptions from the selected simulation. Financial data and generated reports are not transmitted to WARPSimLab or stored by an external service.

To generate reports from your own simulations, visit the downloads page.
