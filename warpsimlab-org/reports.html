Reports

WARPSimLab reports collect software development notes, research writeups, and educational articles related to personal finance simulation, retirement modeling, and the development of the simulator itself.

Reports are intended for education, exploration, and project transparency. They do not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, or retirement advice.
